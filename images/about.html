Background about the project:
A CMU x Zazzle Collaboration

Zazzle's Mission

Zazzle's mission is to become a destination where anyone can make anything. Zazzle wants to transition from an event-driven platform to an online destination for creativity. Zazzle also wants to attract younger audiences to their platform.

Our Vision

We want to help millennials develop creative habits in their daily lives. Given the right tools and environment, we believe that everyone can be creative. We want to help Zazzle create a suite of apps that helps Zazzle embed itself into the daily lives of everyday creators.

ProposedStrategy

We developed a framework for creative habit formation. This framework may be used to generate new concept for new products that encourage creative habits. It may also be used to evaluate and improve upon existing product concepts.

Motivation

Does the product address a fundamental human need?

Trigger

Is there a powerful just-in-time trigger that taps into a fundamental motivation?

Creative Act

Is there a creative act at the heart of the product?

Reward

Is there a meaningful, variable reward at the end of the act? Does the reward tie back in with the user’s original motivation?

Investment

Does the product invite the user to invest more information/resources/attention over time?
